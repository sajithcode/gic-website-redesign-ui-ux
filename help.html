Help & Support

We are here to assist you 7 days a week, 365 days a year. Find answers to common questions below.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the operating hours for the 1919 hotline?

The 1919 hotline operates from 8.00 am to 8.00 pm, 365 days of the year, providing support in Sinhala, Tamil, and English.

How do I translate a page?

Simply click the Sinhala, Tamil, or English buttons located at the top right of the navigation bar to instantly translate the entire page.

Are all government services available online?

Many services have digital forms available on our Gov Forms page. However, some critical processes (like fingerprinting for passports) still require an in-person visit.
